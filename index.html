Why Me

During my 7-year experience in banking, I have implemented end-to-end system automation projects with the engineering team multiple times which gave me great opportunities to observe software engineers work and realize how much I love and enjoy solving technical problems.

After finishing Harvard CS50 and a few Udemy courses in my spare time, it confirms that solving technical problems and building meaningful products are my passions. So I decided to leave my comfort zone and pursue this career change with App Academy where I learned to utilize Javascript, React, Redux, Python, Flask, SQL, Express, HTML and CSS building a website from back-end to front-end and everything in between.

Even though software engineer is very different from my previous finance job, I believe I can bring the best from the 2 worlds together to help company improve people's financial life!
